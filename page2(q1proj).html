\ Noli me Tangere Characters

Characters of Noli Me Tangere
Don Anastacio(Pilosopotasyo)
  • An old man who used to study philosophy and who prefers secular knowledge to Catholicism
  • Called a "madman" or in tagalog "Pilosopotacio"
  • Seen as "insane" by the lower classes or uneducated people
  • Seen as "smart" by the ones that have the privilege to have quality education
Victorina de los Reyes de de Espadaña(Doña Victorina)
  • Wife ofDon Tiburcio
  • Friend of Kapitan Tiago
  • Sought to associate herself with the influential figures of San Diego in order to gain further social status
  • Pretends to be a Spaniard despite being a native Indian or Indio
  • A domineering person, committed to do anything to gain self-esteem
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin(Crisostomo Ibarra)
  • The son of Don Ibarra or Don Rafael Ibarra
  • Studied in Europe for over 7 years
  • Fiancee of Maria Clara
  • Believed in the power of education to enact reforms and made efforts to establish a school in San Diego
Maria Clara de los Santos(Maria Clara)
  • Daughter of Capitan Tiago
  • Known for being a kindhearted young woman
  • Raised by Capitan Tiago but originally Padre Damaso being the father
  • Studied in the convent of Sta. Clara
Narcisa(Sisa)
  • Mother of Basilio and Crispin
  • Constantly abused by her husband, Pedro who is a gambler
  • Caring but submissive woman, a very common trait among women who are considered to be "babaeng tagalog"
